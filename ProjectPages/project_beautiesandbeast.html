Beauties and Beast

Beauties and Beast is a virtual reality (VR) first person shooting (FPS) game.
In this game, player can use different weapons and strategies, and work closely with his three beautiful allies - a gunner, a rancher, and a mage to fight against an evil and powerful dragon.

Three Beauties


The dragon is designed to have two attack modes - biting and casting fireball.
The former is a more powerful melee attack while the latter is a ranged attack.

Dragon’s Melee Attack – Biting

Dragon’s Ranged Attack – Casting Fireball


Player can change weapon via the in-game menu, which takes the anime Sword Art Online as the reference and is of a tree structure rendered on top of other game objects.

In-Game Menu

The game was developed with Unity and Oculus Rift.
